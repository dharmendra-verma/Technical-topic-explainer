🧠 How to Memorize & Apply Data Points START HERE ▼
Rule 1 Use Round Numbers. Don't memorize $8,098,730,121 — say ~$8 Billion. Interviewers care about order of magnitude, not precision.
Rule 2 Anchor to a Framework. Always link the number to a core PM question. Example: "With ~80% US smartphone penetration vs ~45% India, an offline-first strategy captures the other 55%."
Rule 3 Periodically Update. Review anchor points every 6-12 months. Tech numbers shift fast.

👁️ The Attention Economy CONCEPT ▼

An economic model where human attention is the scarce, most valuable resource. Information is infinite and free — but human attention is finite (~24 hrs/day).

"In the attention economy, if you're not paying for the product, you are the product — your attention is being packaged and sold to advertisers."

How Big Tech Monetises Attention

Key Characteristics

"In an information-rich world, the wealth of information means a dearth of something else... it consumes the attention of its recipients. A wealth of information creates a poverty of attention." — Herbert Simon (1971, Nobel Laureate)
